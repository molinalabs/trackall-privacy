Privacy Policy for TrackAll: Packages & Orders Tracking List

Last Updated: December 29, 2025

Overview

TrackAll ("we", "our", or "the extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

Data We Collect

Website Content

TrackAll collects and processes website content from tracking URLs that you manually add to the extension. Specifically:

  • Tracking Page Content: When you add a tracking URL, the extension fetches the HTML content of that page to:
    • Extract the page title or Open Graph title to automatically populate the product name
    • Analyze page text to detect delivery status keywords (e.g., "delivered", "in transit", "out for delivery")
    • Monitor status changes during automatic checks
  • Tracking URLs: The URLs you provide for package tracking are stored locally on your device
  • Product Names: Either provided by you or automatically extracted from the tracking page title
  • Delivery Status: Status information detected from tracking pages (In Transit, Out for Delivery, Delivered)
  • Timestamps: Last update time for each tracked package

What We DO NOT Collect

TrackAll does NOT collect:

  • ❌ Personal identifying information (name, email, address, phone number)
  • ❌ Browsing history or web activity outside of tracking URLs you add
  • ❌ Financial or payment information
  • ❌ Authentication credentials or passwords
  • ❌ Location data or IP addresses
  • ❌ User behavior, clicks, or analytics
  • ❌ Health information
  • ❌ Communications or messages

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for package tracking functionality:

  1. Display Tracking Information: Show your tracked packages and their current status
  2. Automatic Status Updates: Periodically check tracking URLs (every 2 hours) to detect status changes
  3. Notifications: Alert you when package status changes
  4. Product Name Auto-Fill: Extract page titles to automatically name your packages

Data Storage and Security

Local Storage Only

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's sync storage
  • No external servers: We do not transmit, upload, or store your data on any external servers
  • No third-party sharing: Your data is never shared with, sold to, or accessed by third parties
  • Chrome Sync: If you use Chrome Sync, your tracking data syncs across your Chrome browsers where you're signed in (this is handled entirely by Google Chrome)

Data Retention

  • Tracking data remains on your device until you remove it
  • Delivered packages are automatically removed 24 hours after delivery
  • You can manually remove any package at any time
  • Uninstalling the extension deletes all stored data

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Add/Remove Packages: Manage your tracked packages at any time
  • Clear All Data: Uninstall the extension to remove all data
  • No Account Required: No registration or account creation needed
  • Opt-Out of Notifications: Disable notifications in Chrome settings

Third-Party Services

TrackAll does NOT use:

  • ❌ Analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • ❌ Advertising networks
  • ❌ Tracking pixels or cookies
  • ❌ External APIs or services
  • ❌ Social media integrations

The extension directly accesses only the tracking URLs you provide.

Permissions Explanation

TrackAll requires the following Chrome permissions:

Storage Permission

  • Purpose: Save your tracking data locally on your device
  • Data Stored: Tracking URLs, product names, delivery status, timestamps

Alarms Permission

  • Purpose: Schedule automatic status checks every 2 hours
  • Usage: Wake the extension to check for delivery updates

Notifications Permission

  • Purpose: Alert you when package status changes
  • Usage: Display browser notifications for status updates

Host Permissions (Access to All URLs)

  • Purpose: Access tracking pages from any carrier or retailer
  • Limitation: Only accesses URLs you explicitly add to the extension
  • Why Needed: Tracking URLs vary across carriers (USPS, UPS, FedEx, Amazon, DHL, etc.)

Children's Privacy

TrackAll does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The extension is intended for general audiences and does not target children.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the extension or legal requirements. Changes will be posted with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Data Processing for EU Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union:

  • Legal Basis: Consent (by using the extension) and legitimate interest (providing package tracking functionality)
  • Data Controller: The extension processes data locally on your device; no external controller
  • Your Rights: Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection
  • Data Location: All data remains on your device; not transferred outside the EU

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident:

  • We do not sell your personal information
  • You have the right to know what data we collect (listed above)
  • You have the right to delete your data (uninstall extension)
  • You have the right to opt-out (don't use the extension)
